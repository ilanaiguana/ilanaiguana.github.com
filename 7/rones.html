Ramel Rones

Ramel (Rami) Rones is a master Tai Chi instructor in the Boston area. He does research about Tai Chi and Western science at Tufts, Dana Farber and the Boston Medical Center. He teaches mind-body health privately and classes.

He began studying Tai Chi in Jerusalem in 1984 and came to Boston in 1996 to study with a renown Chinese author and teacher. After winning Gold medals and performing across China on the USA-Canadan martial team he turned his focus towards health and healing.

He works with cancer, arthritis, fibromyalgia, or anyone seeking better mental and physical health.

His best selling books have won awards, he has appeared on CNN, National Geographic, at the science museum, and at schools and universitys around the world.
